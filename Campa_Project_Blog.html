Latest Blog

April 15, 2021

While packing my child's lunchbox for school, I don't usually leave treats. But he recently discovered my oatmeal cookies sandwiches when I made some for a customer about two weeks ago. I had only made a batch of cookies similar to this type once before, and as I am always baking different items, one style never sticks around too long!

I've christened this type of sandwich cookie the "Biscoff Butter Sammy", if you'd like to try one at our bakery. As for treats, I don't feel so bad leaving these in his lunchbox once or twice a week because there is a whole serving of oatmeal for every 2 sammy's.
(but don't tell him that!)

- Keiko O'Brien, Head Patissier
